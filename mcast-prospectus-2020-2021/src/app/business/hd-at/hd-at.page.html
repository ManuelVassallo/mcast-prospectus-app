Higher Diploma for Accounting Technicians

MQF Level Level 5 Course Duration 2 Year Full-time Entry Requirements MCAST Advanced Diploma in Accounting Course Description This course builds upon the MCAST Advanced Diploma in Accounting and offers learners the possibility to become members of the Association of Accounting Technicians (UK). During this programme, the learners will continue to broaden and deepen their studies in the different areas of accounting including Financial and Management Accounting as well as Management and Business Law. Learners who successfully complete the AAT (UK) Advanced Diploma in Accounting as well as the AAT (UK) Professional Diploma in Accounting, are entitled for exemptions from the ACCA qualification (F1, F2, F3) Through successful completion of related study-units, leaners will also be eligible to register for further exemptions namely, F4 and F5.
