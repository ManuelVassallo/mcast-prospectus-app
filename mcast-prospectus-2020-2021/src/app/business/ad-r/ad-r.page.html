Advanced Diploma in Retail

MQF Level Level 4 Course Duration 2 Year Full-time Entry Requirements This course aims to introduce learners to diverse world of retail. Learners will be exposed at the different aspects and career opportunities related to retail inculding front line, logistics, purchasing, retail operations and visiual merchandising as an example. The retail industry in Malta is one of the largest industries and offers career opportunities with both local as well as international chains, and this course allows learners to be exposed to different careers related to the retail industry. Learners will be covering all aspects of the retail environment including Stock Presentation, Customer Care, Buying, Visual Merchandising and Consumer Behaviour, and e-commerce. Throughout this course, which is on Apprenticeship, learners will be studying on a dual system whereby they will have days at school, while they will be having days out working in industry.
