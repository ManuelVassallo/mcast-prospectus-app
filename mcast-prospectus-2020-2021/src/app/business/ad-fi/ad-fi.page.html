Advanced Diploma in Finance and Insurance

MQF Level Level 4 Course Duration 2 Year Full-time Entry Requirements MCAST Diploma in Business or 4 SEC/O-Level /SSC&P (Level 3) passes Compulsory: English Language and Mathematics Course Description This course as with all other level four courses within the Institute of Business Management and Commerce is on apprenticeship and follows a dual system whereby learners as from the 2nd semester of their first year of studies will be spending days at school as well as days working in industry.
