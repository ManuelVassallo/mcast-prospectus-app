Institute of Applied Science

Applied Science relates to the application of scientific knowledge to practical challenges and jobs. At the Institute of Applied Sciences, learners are prepared for technological careers within the health, pharmaceutical, environmental, food and beverage, or general engineering sectors. Students learn to appreciate how the fundamental principles of science relate to the technological operations of the workplace, while developing the skills to work in laboratories, hospitals, farms, and with environmental technologies and methodologies, as well as to apply basic scientific principles in industrial settings.
