Advanced Diploma in Applied Science

MQF Level Level 4 Course Duration 2 Year Full-time Entry Requirements MCAST Diploma in Applied Science or 4 SEC/O-Level/SSC&P (Level 3) from English Language, Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Design and Technology, Health and Social Care Course Description This course of studies is aimed at learners who wish to embark on a scientific career in a range of industries such as petrochemical, life sciences, health, pharmaceutics, and environment. The programme of studies contains a wide range of science and technology study-units that reflect aspects of employment within relevant industries. The learner will appreciate how the fundamental principles of science relate to the technological operations of the workplace. They will develop the necessary skills to work in a laboratory environment within the manufacturing industry, and to apply basic principles within the workplace.
