Foundation Certificate in Applied Science

MQF Level Level 1 Course Duration 1 Year Full-time Entry Requirements Finished Compulsory Education Initial Assessment Tests (as may be applicable) Course Description This course of studies aims to provide the learner with the basic knowledge of science and technology and how these apply to everyday life. The study units deal with the different disciplines of science hence giving the learner an overview of all the main scientific concepts necessary as a basis for further vocational studies within the science sector.
