Foundation Certificate in Horticulture and Animal Care

MQF Level Level 2 Course Duration 1 Year Full-time Entry Requirements Finished Compulsory Education or MCAST Introductory Certificate Initial Assessment Tests (as may be applicable) Course Description This course of studies is designed to equip learners with a range of basic skills and competencies enabling them to become competent in various land-based activities related to the agricultural sector. This programme of studies includes a significant amount of vocational practice that will provide a clear idea of the nature of the area that the learner may wish to follow in the future. Learners will have the opportunity to gain handson experience with farm animals, exotic animals and plants. Special focus will be given to soil, crop and animal management, livestock nutrition and farm maintenance.
