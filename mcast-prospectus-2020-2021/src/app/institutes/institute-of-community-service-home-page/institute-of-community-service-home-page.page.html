Institute of Community Service

The Institute of Community Services equips learners with the required holistic skills in various fields for the wellbeing of our community. In particular we offer coursesin Sports, Health and Social Care, Early Years, Beauty, Hairdressing, Inclusive Education and Protection, Security and Enforcement. Our vocational educators amalgamate theory and practice to provide a hands on experience to our learners which reflects the workplace and its requirements within industry. Our institute is equipped with a number of resources to enable this approach to learning, such as fullyfunctional hair salons, beauty salons and Care rooms. Furthermore, our VET programmes include a work placement which offers valuable experience withinthe industry. Courses offered range from MQF level 1 to level 6
