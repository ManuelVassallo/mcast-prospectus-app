Gozo Campus


The MCAST Gozo Campus offers a selection of 24 fulltime day courses ranging from MQF Levels 1 to 6 and covering a broad spectrum of vocational disciplines. Some of these programmes lead learners directly to employment, whist others lead to higher level studies in Gozo and/or Malta. In either case, learners are thoroughly prepared for their next step, whichever that may be. MCAST training programmes delivered at the GozoCampus fulfil the same quality assurance standards that are applicable to the same programmes delivered at the respective MCAST Institutes in Malta, ensuring that Gozitan students benefit from the same quality of service on their home ground. Learners hailing from the northern region of Malta who choose to study at the Gozo Campus are eligible for a government subsidy.
