Bachelor of Arts (Honours) in Fashion

MQF Level Level 6 Course Duration 3 Year Full-time Entry Requirements MCAST Advanced Diploma in Fashion and Retail MCAST Advanced Diploma in Art and Design with a textiles major project City and Guilds Advanced Certificate in Tailoring or 2 A-Level passes and 2 I-Level passes Compulsory A-Level: Art, or Graphical Communication, or Engineering Drawing, or Physics Applicants may be asked to sit for an interview and/or present their Portfolio. Course Description This degree aims to respond to the demand for fashion professionals within the fashion industry, including fashion/costume design and production, retail and marketing. Supported with contextual studies to understand how fashion designers inspire their creativity from epic eras of fashion. The course provides learners with the specialist skills to create patterns using traditional techniques as well as modern software to develop CAD/CAM within the fashion production process on small and mass production scales. Moreover, the course offers specialist units addressing the retail and marketing content to equip learners with the necessary skills for the industry needs. The course is targeted towards both individuals who wish to set up their own firm with the required entrepreneurial skills, and those who wish to progress in the vast fashion business
