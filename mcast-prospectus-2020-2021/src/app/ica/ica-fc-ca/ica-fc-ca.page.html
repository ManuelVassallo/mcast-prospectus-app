Foundation Certificate in Creative ARts

MQF Level Level 2 Course Duration 1 Year Full-time Entry Requirements Finished Compulsory Education or MCAST Introductory Certificate Initial Assessment Tests (as may be applicable) Course Description This programme of studies provides an opportunity to learners, in possession of the Secondary School Certificate and Profile (SSC&P), to follow a structured programme that explores the basics of various Creative Art disciplines, namely Art, Design, Media and Performing Arts. The programme includes a significant amount of vocational practice, as well as essential key skills subjects that will help learners form a clear idea of the nature of the vocation they intend to follow at higher levels while, at the same time, developing their competences within basic areas such as language, numeracy, information technology, science and individual and social responsibility.
