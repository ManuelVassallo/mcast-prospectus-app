Advanced Diploma in Welding and Fabrication

MQF Level Level 4 Course Duration 3 Year Full-time Entry Requirements MCAST Diploma in Welding and Fabrication or MCAST Diploma in Construction Engineering or MCAST Diploma in Automotive Repair (Body and Paint) or MCAST Diploma in Automotive Maintenance and Repair or MCAST Diploma in Mechanical Engineering or MCAST Diploma in Aircraft Structures and Repairs or MCAST Diploma in Engineering (Electronics) or MCAST Diploma in Electrical Installations or 4 SEC/O-Level/SSC&P (Level 3) passes Preferred: English, Mathematics, Engineering Technology, Technical Drawing, Graphical Communication or Design and Technology Course Description This course combines theoretical knowledge and practical training carried out in Collegebased industrial workshops. Learners will be expected to participate individually and in teams to fabricate welded products. They will learn how to analyse and generate solutions related to typical fabrication using thin plates and pipes. Learners will also be given the opportunity to follow an apprenticeship to improve their handson experience. In order to be able to appreciate the importance of good production management, supervised visits to complex production setups are organised during the course. Applicants need to be able to work within the industries concerned. Applicants coming in through the Secondary Education Certificate route may be requested to complete an additional Level 4 preparatory year if they do not already have prior exposure and experience in the sector
