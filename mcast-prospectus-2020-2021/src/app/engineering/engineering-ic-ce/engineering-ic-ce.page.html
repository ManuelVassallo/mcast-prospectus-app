Introductory Certificate in Construction Engineering

MQF Level Level 1 Course Duration 1 Year Full-time Entry Requirements Finished Compulsory Education Course Description The programme provides an opportunity for prospective learners in possession of the Secondary School Certificate and Profile (SSC&P) to experience and train in the basic skills related to the Construction industry. It presents learners with an opportunity to have extensive vocational hands on practice in various sectors. The course includes practical hands on woodwork, plumbing, sheet-metal, bench-fitting, electrical and trowel practical exercises. The course includes a significant key skills component that will help learners improve their communication, literacy and numeracy skills. IT and Individual and Social Responsibility sessions are also part of the key skills component.
