Course Description This course of studies is one of the most sought-after within this Institute. It is highly popular among prospective candidates because it provides ample opportunities to find employment at the end of the two-year programme.The studyunits are designed in such a manner to provide a strong link between theoretical concepts and hands-on practice during the work placements. Lectures focus on guiding the undergraduates how to make learning for young children stimulating and fun, in a safe and healthy environment. Upon completion of this programme, the undergraduates may proceed to the final year of the Bachelor of Arts (Hons) in Early Childhood Education and Care (ECEC) within the Faculty of Education, University of Malta, subject to fulfilling a number of requirements relating to proficiency in Maltese and English language
