Foundation Certificate in Sports

MQF Level Level 2 Course Duration 1 Year Full-time Entry Requirements MCAST Diploma in Applied Science or 4 SEC/O-Level/SSC&P (Level 3) from English Language, Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Design and Technology, Health and Social Care Course Description This programme of studies serves as a stepping stone to a future working career in the sport sector. The learner experiences the practical and basic theoretical components of a number of sports- and fitnessrelated subjects. The vocational study-units cover the basics of health and active lifestyles, community sports, fitness training methods and introduction to gym-based exercise. Concurrently, learners strengthen their knowledge of the key skills in English, Maltese, Mathematics, Science, Information Technology, and Individual and Social Responsibility. Throughout the academic year, MCAST and the Institute of Community Services organise a number of sport-related extra-curricular activities during which the learners will be invited to participate in order to enrich their experience on campus.
