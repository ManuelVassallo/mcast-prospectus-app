Advanced Diploma in Beauty and Complementary Therapies

MQF Level Level 4 Course Duration 2 Year Full-time Entry Requirements ITEC Diploma for Beauty Specialists or MCAST Diploma for Beauty Specialists Course Description This programme is made up of study-units intended to equip learners with the knowledge to provide treatments for the purpose of relaxation and stress-relief as well as to improve the condition of the skin. Learners will have the opportunity to perform practical tasks during hands-on sessions within the MCAST salons. There will also be work placement periods within established Salons and Spas. Study units during the second year of studies include alternative therapies such as reflexology, stone therapy and aromatherapy. At the end of the first academic year, successful learners will have the option to exit their studies, and be awarded the MCAST Award in Beauty Therapy. This award will enable them to work in the industry as beauty therapists
